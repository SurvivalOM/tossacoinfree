Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. This site is operated by Future Proofing Lifestyle.

We respect your privacy and collect as little as possible. This policy explains what happens when you use this coin flip website.

Information we store

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect names, emails, or personal details. Your flip history, running Heads/Tails tally, and your chosen coin colour are saved only in your own browser using local storage on your device. You can erase them any time with the "Clear" button, or by clearing your browser data.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display advertising to keep it free. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more on how advertising partners use data, see How Google uses information from sites that use its services. Where required, an on-page consent notice will ask for your permission before non-essential cookies are set.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic (such as how many people visit). This data is not used to identify individuals.

Children

This is a general-audience tool and is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Your choices

You can use the tool with cookies blocked; the coin flip itself works without them. You can clear your on-device history at any time.
